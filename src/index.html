Welcome to Mono.Chat - Legacy Chatroom

Step back in time and experience the nostalgia of online chat from the year 2000 with Mono.Chat.

Chat Rooms

Join our chat rooms and connect with people from around the world. Share your thoughts, make new friends, and relive the excitement of online conversations.

System Requirements

To fully enjoy the legacy version of Mono.Chat, you need to be using a 32 bit or 64 bit version of Windows with a 32 bit browser.

Some examples are:

  • Internet Explorer 3 and above (recommended)
  • Pale Moon
  • Google Chrome 44 or below (Chrome 42+ has NPAPI disabled by default)
  • Apple Safari
  • Netscape Navigator 3 and above
  • SeaMonkey 2.49.5 or below
  • Opera 36 and below
  • Firefox 52 or below (Firefox 52 has NPAPI disabled by default)

Internet Explorer

For Internet Explorer users, you may receive a dialog asking if you would like to run the MSN Chat Control, signed by Microsoft Corporation MSN. You'll need to click "Yes" or "Allow" to continue.

NPAPI Browser

For browsers with NPAPI support, download and extract the NPPGWrap.dll plugin to your browser's plugin folder, then restart your browser and return to this page. Click here to download the plugin.
